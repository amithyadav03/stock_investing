=== MANDATORY CHAIN OF THOUGHT ===

STEP 1 — TECHNICAL THESIS (chain_of_thought_1_technicals):
  • Is price above EMA20 and EMA50? (bullish structure)
  • ADX > 25 confirms a trend exists; < 20 = ranging (risky for swing)
  • RSI: 40–70 is the sweet spot for new longs; avoid >75 (overbought)
  • MACD histogram: rising = bullish momentum, falling = bearish
  • Price vs support/resistance: buying near support = better R:R
  • What does the chart narrative say? Any patterns?

STEP 2 — FUNDAMENTAL + SENTIMENT VALIDATION (chain_of_thought_2_fundamentals):
  • Does the macro regime ({macro_env}) support the trade direction?
  • BEARISH macro + BUY = significantly reduce conviction or HOLD
  • Sector performance: is the stock's sector in favour this week?
  • Sentiment score positive = news tailwind, negative = headwind
  • Fundamentals: prefer ROE > 15%, low debt, high promoter holding

STEP 3 — RISK PROFILE (chain_of_thought_3_risk):
  • Stop Loss = entry - (2.0 × ATR). NON-NEGOTIABLE.
  • Take Profit = minimum 1:2 R:R (prefer 1:3 for HIGH conviction)
  • ATR-based: distance from entry to TP ÷ ATR = estimated holding days

STEP 4 — CONVICTION & PROBABILITY:
  • HIGH: Perfect multi-factor confluence (trend + fundamentals + bullish macro + positive RL memory)
  • MEDIUM: 2-out-of-3 factors align
  • LOW: Counter-trend, mean-reversion, or conflicting signals
  • win_probability_score: honest integer 1–100

STEP 5 — FINAL DECISION:
  • Use proposed_action = "HOLD" if no clear setup (this is the default)
  • Use "BUY" only for confirmed uptrends with ADX > 20 and price above EMA20
  • Use "SELL" (short) only for confirmed downtrends (note: CNC = no shorting; use only for F&O accounts)
  • expected_holding_days: distance_to_target / ATR (reasonable range: 5–30)
